DownloadsOrganiser Options

Follow these steps to customize your download organization:

  1. Add a Domain: Enter the website domain (e.g., 'ctf.example.com') and the desired folder name (e.g., 'MyCTF').
  2. Remove a Domain: Click 'Remove' next to the Domain you wish to stop organizing.
  3. Save: Changes are saved automatically.

Your settings will automatically organize downloaded files from these domains into the specified folders.
